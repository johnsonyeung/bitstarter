We intended to develop a website for people who recalled the past memory to his /her most beloved persons. The users can put the pictures or videos in this website.

Some people may want to visit the Tombs of his/her ancestor. However, it is far away from where they are living in. Under this condition, he /she can visit this website to recall his/her memory with those belove picture (slide showing). There are some icon, such a bouquet of flower to put into the web when he/she visited there.

With respect different religious, the user can set different songs for the ancestor related to.

The system also will send the notice in email or SMS to users to alert him/her the memorable date for that ancestor. He can take time to visit the tomb in actual also. The website will also link the detail of beloved idols. It can help to attract some potential users visiting this website. It will also hyperlink to Youtube, Wikipedia.org or related topic's website.

The financial support of this website is from the user's donation and the advertising. We hope we can breakeven after 1.5 years.
